Privacy Policy for Todo App

Last updated: April 5, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Todo App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Task data (titles, descriptions, due dates, priorities)
  • Category information
  • Attachments (images, documents)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Authenticate users
  • Sync data across devices
  • Improve app performance
  • Send notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Firebase Services

We use the following Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For user authentication
  • Firebase Firestore: For storing task data
  • Firebase Storage: For storing attachments
  • Firebase Analytics: For app usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting

4.2 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls

5. Data Retention

  • Task data: Retained until user deletion
  • Account information: Retained until account deletion
  • Analytics data: Retained for 14 months

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of analytics

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
